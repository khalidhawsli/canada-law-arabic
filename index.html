Arabic speakers in Canada

Legal knowledge shouldn't depend on the language you speak

Over 600,000 Arabic speakers live in Canada, yet virtually no free, credible Arabic‑language legal education existed. We close that gap with free content in Arabic, English, and French.

Delivered by a lawyer. Founder Khalid Alhawasli, LL.M. (Osgoode Hall), pursuing Alberta licensure.
⚠️ Educational content only. Nothing on this site is legal advice. Consult a licensed lawyer in your province for advice about your specific situation.
